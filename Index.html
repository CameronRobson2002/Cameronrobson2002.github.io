Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy is centered around creating an inclusive and engaging learning environment. I empoly positive pedagogy and resourses in my teaching.

As a teacher, my philosophy centers on creating a positive, inclusive classroom environment where every student feels valued and motivated to succeed. I believe that building strong relationships with my students is key to understanding their unique needs and fostering a sense of belonging. I strive to cultivate a growth mindset, encouraging students to embrace challenges and view mistakes as opportunities for learning.

I am committed to differentiating my instruction to meet the diverse needs of my students, ensuring that each child has access to the support and resources they need to thrive. I use interactive and hands-on activities to actively engage my students, promoting critical thinking and problem-solving skills. By fostering a culture of respect, empathy, and collaboration, I aim to create a classroom where students feel safe to express themselves, take risks, and develop a lifelong love of learning.
